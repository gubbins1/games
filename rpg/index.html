Comparison of Ruleset Pros and Cons

The ideal ruleset for trying out GURPs:

  • Uses the revised edition GURPS rules
  • Only covers low-tech rules
  • Does not cover any magic rules
  • Requires reading few pages to get started playing
  • Basic rules are presented concisely and without any distracting references to advanced combat options
  • Presents advanced topics (missile weapons, wound recovery, close combat, etc.) as options to be learned after players are comfortable with the basics
  • Equipment lists and character customization options (skills, advantages, etc.) limited to those specific to arena combat
  • Includes a variety of viable starting characters suitable for arena combat
